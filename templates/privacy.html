Policy

Your financial data is yours. Here is how we handle it. Last updated: April 2026.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the service, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is needed to operate your account.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expenses, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and enabling filtering by date range. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the features you use.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account at any time, at which point all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces — this involves a request to Google's servers when you load a page. We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with any third party.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related queries promptly and transparently.

Your data, your control.

We built Spendly to help you, not to profit from your information.
